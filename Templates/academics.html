Grade Programs

We structure our academic offerings to meet the developmental needs of students at every stage. Below are the programs we offer across various grade levels:

  • Primary School (Grades K-5): A solid foundation in reading, writing, math, and social studies. We focus on building curiosity and fundamental skills that serve as the base for future learning.
  • Middle School (Grades 6-8): A transitional phase where students explore different subjects, develop critical thinking, and enhance their social and emotional skills. We also introduce students to elective courses to encourage deeper exploration of personal interests.
  • High School (Grades 9-12): A challenging and comprehensive program with college-preparatory courses, Advanced Placement (AP) options, and electives in the arts, STEM, and humanities. We prepare students for higher education and their future careers through a well-rounded academic approach.

Specialized Academic Programs

We believe in providing opportunities for students to dive deeper into their academic passions. Our specialized programs include:

  • STEM Program: Focused on science, technology, engineering, and mathematics, this program encourages critical thinking, problem-solving, and innovation through hands-on projects and real-world applications.
  • International Baccalaureate (IB) Program: A globally recognized program that fosters inquiry-based learning, intercultural understanding, and academic excellence. Our IB program is designed to develop well-rounded students with the skills to excel in a globalized world.
  • Arts Program: Students can explore their creative side through specialized courses in visual arts, performing arts, and music. The arts program encourages self-expression and helps students develop discipline and creativity.
  • Language Immersion Program: For students who wish to become fluent in another language, we offer immersive language learning experiences with cultural exchange opportunities and practical language skills development.

Advanced Placement (AP) Courses

Our high school offers a wide variety of Advanced Placement (AP) courses for students who are ready to take on college-level material. These courses provide opportunities for students to earn college credit while still in high school and help prepare them for the academic rigor of higher education.

Some of the AP courses we offer include:

  • AP Calculus
  • AP Biology
  • AP English Language and Composition
  • AP U.S. History
  • AP Computer Science
  • AP Psychology
  • AP Environmental Science
