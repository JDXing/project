About Us

Where Excellence Meets Innovation

Welcome to Cherupushpam U.P. School, Therthally, an unaided Christian minority institution founded in 1970 under the Sanjos Province of the Missionary Sisters of Mary Immaculate (MSMI) Congregation, Kannur.

Guided by our motto - Truth, Love, and Service - we are dedicated to providing comprehensive, holistic, and value-based education. We follow CBSE syllabus for LP and State syllabus for UP, ensuring quality education that nurtures academic excellence and character formation.

Our school offers smart classrooms, spoken English classes, library, IT lab, playgrounds, and school bus facilities. We encourage students to explore their talents through arts, sports, quizzes, cultural and innovative programs.

Together, we prepare students to become responsible, patriotic, and compassionate citizens who contribute to society with love and integrity.

Our History

Cherupushpam U.P. School, Therthally is an unaided Christian Minority School functioning under the direction and control of the Sanjos Province of the Missionary Sisters of Mary Immaculate (MSMI) Congregation, Kannur, Kerala. MSMI, a Christian Religious Congregation founded in 1962 by Rev. Msgr. C.J. Varkey, a great visionary, is renowned in India and abroad for its valuable contribution in various fields. The Congregation manages numerous well-established centres including schools, hospitals, media and communication centres, homes for the aged, counselling centres, retreat centres, social welfare projects, and training institutes. Its services extend across Kerala, Karnataka, Tamil Nadu, Andhra Pradesh, Uttar Pradesh, Delhi, Punjab, Jammu, Madhya Pradesh, Bhopal, Maharashtra, and several parts of the world. Cherupushpam U.P. School, Therthally, is a co-educational institution offering CBSE syllabus for LP section and State syllabus for UP section. The school is open to children of all communities and aims to provide quality education to all irrespective of caste, creed, or religion. Guided by the motto “Truth, Love and Service,” the institution strives to create intellectually sound, emotionally mature, morally upright, socially responsible, and spiritually inspired individuals who can contribute positively to society and the nation.

ST. LITTLE FLOWER (Patron Saint of the School)

St. Little Flower, also known as St. Thérèse of the Child Jesus, is the heavenly patron of our school. She was a young Carmelite nun who lived a life of deep simplicity, humility, and love for God.

St. Thérèse is known for her “Little Way” - finding holiness in small acts of kindness, love, and sacrifice done with a pure heart. Her life reminds us that greatness is achieved not by doing extraordinary things, but by doing ordinary things with extraordinary love.

As the patron of our school, St. Little Flower inspires every child to grow with a pure heart, live joyfully, and spread love and service to all. We believe her blessings guide our students to become gentle, compassionate, and value-filled human beings.
