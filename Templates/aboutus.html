About Us

Where Excellence Meets Innovation

Welcome to Cherupushpam U.P. School, Therthally - a place where learning is inspired by Truth, Love, and Service. Founded in 1970, our school is an unaided Christian minority institution operating under the direction and control of the Sanjos Province of the Missionary Sisters of Mary Immaculate (MSMI) Congregation, Kannur.

We offer CBSE syllabus for LP classes and State syllabus for UP classes, ensuring comprehensive and inclusive education for every child.

Our Vision

To provide comprehensive, holistic, and value-based education that empowers each student as a human person and contributes to the empowerment of our nation through their valuable service.

Our Mission

To impart value-based, effective education that helps students become productive and patriotic citizens.

To enhance and promote skills that make students self-reliant, competent, and capable of meeting global challenges.

To guide students to become true human beings, respect humanity, and recognize the Divine presence within and around them.

ST. LITTLE FLOWER (Patron Saint of the School)

St. Little Flower, also known as St. Thérèse of the Child Jesus, is the heavenly patron of our school. She was a young Carmelite nun who lived a life of deep simplicity, humility, and love for God.

St. Thérèse is known for her “Little Way” - finding holiness in small acts of kindness, love, and sacrifice done with a pure heart. Her life reminds us that greatness is achieved not by doing extraordinary things, but by doing ordinary things with extraordinary love.

As the patron of our school, St. Little Flower inspires every child to grow with a pure heart, live joyfully, and spread love and service to all. We believe her blessings guide our students to become gentle, compassionate, and value-filled human beings.

Our Clubs

At Cherupushpam U.P. School, Therthally, we believe in the holistic development of every child. Our school clubs encourage students to discover their talents, think creatively, and work together as a team.

1. Vidhyarangam Kalasahithya Vedhi

A vibrant platform that nurtures creativity and artistic talents. Students get opportunities to participate in poetry, story writing, drama, and cultural activities that enrich their imagination and expression.

2. Maths Club

The Maths Club makes learning mathematics fun through puzzles, games, quizzes, and activities that strengthen problem-solving skills and logical thinking.

3. English Club

This club improves communication skills through debates, storytelling, elocution, drama, and creative writing activities, helping students gain confidence in using English.

4. Science Club

The Science Club promotes curiosity and innovation by involving students in experiments, science projects, and exhibitions, encouraging scientific thinking.

5. Social Science Club

This club helps students understand society, culture, and history better through role plays, discussions, model-making, and awareness programs on current issues.

6. IT Club

The IT Club equips students with basic computer skills, digital literacy, and exposure to new technologies, preparing them for the digital world.

7. Hindi Club

This club develops love for the Hindi language through storytelling, poetry recitation, skits, and competitions that enhance language proficiency.
