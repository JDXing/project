About Us

Where Excellence Meets Innovation

Welcome to Cherupushpam U.P. School, Therthally - a place where learning is inspired by Truth, Love, and Service. Founded in 1970, our school is an unaided Christian minority institution operating under the direction and control of the Sanjos Province of the Missionary Sisters of Mary Immaculate (MSMI) Congregation, Kannur.

We offer CBSE syllabus for LP classes and State syllabus for UP classes, ensuring comprehensive and inclusive education for every child.

Our Vision

To provide comprehensive, holistic, and value-based education that empowers each student as a human person and contributes to the empowerment of our nation through their valuable service.

Our Mission

To impart value-based, effective education that helps students become productive and patriotic citizens.

To enhance and promote skills that make students self-reliant, competent, and capable of meeting global challenges.

To guide students to become true human beings, respect humanity, and recognize the Divine presence within and around them.

Our Patron

We are blessed to have St. Little Flower as our heavenly patron, inspiring us to walk the path of simplicity, love, and dedication.

Our Facilities & Activities

Our school is equipped with:

  • Smart classrooms & innovative learning spaces
  • Spoken English classes
  • Library & IT lab
  • Playgrounds for sports & games
  • School bus facilities
  • Morning and evening classes for extra support

We provide opportunities for arts, cultural activities, quizzes, sports, games, and innovative programs, helping every child discover and develop their talents.

At Cherupushpam U.P. School, Therthally, we believe education is not just about academics but about building character, instilling values, and preparing students to serve society with love and integrity.
